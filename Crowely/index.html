Biography

Genre - Hard Rock

CROWLEY is a hard rock band based in Newcastle upon Tyne, UK. Comprised of vocalist Lidya Balaban, guitarists Eliza Lee and Ruth Cranston, bassist Kim Waugh, and drummer Matthew Graham. Formed in 2022, the band aim to reinvigorate the sounds of retro pagan rock.
